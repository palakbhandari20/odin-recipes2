lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

ingredient

  • 2 pounds italian sausage roll
  • 16 lasagna noodles,broken into pieces
  • 2(28 ounch) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2(8 ounch) can tomato sauce
  • 4 cups torn fresh spinach
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese,sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

steps

  1. heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. cook and stir sausage,breaking into smaller pieces, in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. mix noodle into sausage
  2. pour crushed tomatoes and tomato sauce over sausage noodle ixture bring to a boil. cover skillet, reduce heat, and simmer until noodles are cooked and sauce is thickened, about 25 minutes.Add spinach,ricotta cheese,and mozzarella cheese; cover skillet and remove from heat.let lasagna stand for 5 min: top with parmesan cheese.
