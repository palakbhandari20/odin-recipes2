lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

ingredient

  • 1 cucumber ,grated
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp black salt(optional)
  • salt to taste
  • fresh cilantro(for garnish)
  • pinch of red chilli powder

steps

  1. grate the cucumber and squeeze out any excess water.
  2. in a bowl,whisk the yogurt until smooth.
  3. add the grated cucumber to the yogurt
  4. add cumin powder,black salt, and regular salt.mix well
  5. garnish with cilantro leaves and a pinch of red chili powder if desired
  6. serve chilled as a refreshing side dish with rice or any curry
