lasagna

ingredient

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies (optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves (for garnish)
  • 2 tbsp oil

steps

  1. Peel and chop the potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Stir well.
  6. Add the chopped potatoes, mix well, and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add water to cover the potatoes, bring it to a boil, then lower the heat and cover. Cook until potatoes are tender (about 10-15 minutes).
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
